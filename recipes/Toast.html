Egg Avacado Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, lemon juice, or other seasonings

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Mash the avocado in a small bowl and season with salt, pepper, and any optional ingredients.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat and fry the eggs to your liking (sunny-side up or over-easy).
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly on each slice of toast.
  5. Place the fried eggs on top of the avocado on each slice of toast.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
